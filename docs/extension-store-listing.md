# Chrome Web Store Listing

## Title

ContextPassport

## Short Description

Save AI chat context, switch tools or accounts, and resume exactly where you left off.

## Detailed Description

ContextPassport is a universal AI session handoff system for people who build with AI every day. If you hit a usage limit in Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Copilot, or Grok, you should not have to manually reconstruct hours of context, copy partial prompts into notes, or lose momentum when switching to another tool or Google account.

ContextPassport captures the conversation state from the current AI tab, compresses it into a structured Session Passport, and stores it with your authenticated profile. When you open a fresh AI chat, you can inject a clean continuation prompt that explains what was built, which decisions were already made, what files matter, where the work stopped, and what should happen next.

The extension includes:

- Platform-aware content scripts for six major AI platforms
- Secure Google sign-in and backend sync
- A popup dashboard with search, filters, resume actions, stats, and exports
- Auto-capture when a platform limit is detected
- Auto-inject options for faster handoff into a new chat
- Preference controls for compression level, expiry, theme, and data export

ContextPassport is designed for engineers, founders, researchers, and operators who move across multiple AI products and accounts throughout the day. Instead of losing context when limits hit, you keep working.

## Feature List

- Capture active AI conversations as reusable Session Passports
- Resume work across Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Copilot, and Grok
- Search, filter, export, and delete saved sessions
- Secure cloud sync tied to your authenticated identity
- Structured resume prompts with project state, decisions, files, and next steps
